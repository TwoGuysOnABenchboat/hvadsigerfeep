Liminal Brutalis: PA - Update #3 - A colorful approach

February 9, 2026

This sprint I'm working on quite a "colorful" area of the game 
A stark contrast to the otherwise sterile, grey (Brutalistic if you will) world of Liminal Brutalis. But don't be fooled, this place is not for leisure.

Here is a few of the funky assets!

Fun fact! Every single asset uses a single 124x124 index-texture (I know. That's too many pixels).  This of course means that I have to "texture" each asset using color-assigned-UV-islands; which increases the poly-count slightly.

But what we lose in pristine mesh optimization, we gain in draw-calls, texture memory, simplicity and, best of all, fun!

They're are also a ton of fun to make and — In my opinion — gain a lot of personality simply as a consequence of this workflow.

It is very much the same workflow I used when I worked for Brightstar on the MMORPG Embersword > https://www.artstation.com/artwork/4NNQ2L
