Liminal Brutalis: First Devlog (Test)

February 4, 2026

Welcome to the first devlog for Liminal Brutalis. This is where we will share progress, images, and notes as the game evolves.

This is also (mostly) a way to test the new 'Blog' section of the site, and see how it handles multiple posts. So this one is a bit bare-boned. More interesting stuff will follow soon.

What is Liminal Brutalis: PA (Physical Agility)? In short; It's a first-person precision platformer. We are focusing on traversal, rhythm, and crunchy "movement tech".

I grew up on a heavy diet of games rooted in Source. Playing HL1 & HL2, custom traversal maps I developed a taste of that very specific flow of movement. You know which one.

LB:PA Is my love-letter to those games.There are, however, more tricks to LB:PA than just a responsive control-scheme, I'm building a rich world ("The Facility"), soaked in dark lore (For those who care about that) for you to explore and conqure.

In addition to a hand-crafted, detailed world, I wanted LB:PA to stand out a bit from your regular precision jumper. So I'm handing you — wait for it — JUICE! The vending machine juices act as punchy powerups—small boosts that let you chain movement, recover, or push through tougher sections.

These delish packs of goodness can be powerful if used right. While they wont grand you super-powers — and they are not endless — a tiny bit of extra juice at the right time can change a doomed run to a beautiful one. They may also help show the way to... secrets? Oh boy
