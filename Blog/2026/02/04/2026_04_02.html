Liminal Brutalis: First Devlog

February 4, 2026

Welcome to the first devlog for Liminal Brutalis. This is where we will share progress, images, and notes as the game evolves.

The vending machine juices act as punchy powerups—small boosts that let you chain movement, recover, or push through tougher sections.

We are focusing on traversal, rhythm, and crunchy movement tech. More to share soon.
